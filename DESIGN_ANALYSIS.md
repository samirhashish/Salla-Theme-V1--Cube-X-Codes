# Vitrine Furniture Store - Design & CRO Analysis

## Color Scheme
- **Primary Yellow**: #FFC107 (Call-to-action, highlights, banners)
- **Dark Navy Blue**: #1A237E (Footer, buttons, text accents)
- **Neutral Grays**: #F5F5F5, #E0E0E0 (Backgrounds, borders)
- **White**: #FFFFFF (Main background)
- **Dark Text**: #333333, #212121

## Typography
- **Headers**: Bold, modern sans-serif (Poppins/Inter style)
- **Body**: Clean, readable sans-serif
- **Sizes**: Large product titles, medium descriptions, small details

## Key CRO Elements

### 1. Promotional Banner
- **Sticky top banner**: "CLICK HERE for 10% OFF your 1st Order"
- Yellow background with high contrast
- Email capture form with country code selector
- Privacy policy link
- Creates urgency and captures leads

### 2. Product Page Design
- **Hero section**: Large product image gallery with zoom capability
- **Price display**: 
  - Sale price prominently displayed in large font
  - Original price struck through
  - Discount percentage shown (e.g., "Save 60%")
- **Add to Cart**: Large, prominent yellow button
- **Product details**: Clear specifications and features
- **Reviews section**: Customer testimonials build trust
- **Related products**: Cross-sell opportunities

### 3. Cart Page
- **Empty state messaging**: "Your cart is empty" with related products
- **Free shipping threshold**: "Spend EGP 0 more for free shipping"
- **Discount code input**: Encourage coupon usage
- **Customization section**: "Customize your order"
- **Clear pricing breakdown**: Taxes and shipping calculated at checkout

### 4. Cart Drawer (Mini Cart)
- **Quick view**: Shows items without full page navigation
- **Quick actions**: Update quantity, remove items
- **Checkout button**: Prominent call-to-action
- **Discount reminder**: Shows potential savings

### 5. Trust Signals
- **Free shipping badge**: Prominently displayed
- **14-day return policy**: Displayed in header
- **Customer reviews**: Real testimonials with names
- **Payment options**: Multiple payment methods shown
- **Store branches**: Physical locations build credibility

### 6. Navigation & Search
- **Mega menu**: Category-based navigation
- **Search functionality**: Quick product search
- **Breadcrumbs**: Clear navigation path
- **Filter options**: By category, price, etc.

## CRO Best Practices Implemented

1. **Urgency & Scarcity**: Discount percentages, limited-time offers
2. **Social Proof**: Customer reviews and ratings
3. **Clear CTAs**: Yellow buttons stand out
4. **Reduced Friction**: Quick add-to-cart, easy checkout
5. **Trust Building**: Free shipping, return policy, multiple payment options
6. **Mobile Optimization**: Responsive design
7. **High-Quality Imagery**: Professional product photos
8. **Clear Pricing**: No hidden costs, transparent breakdown
9. **Exit Intent**: Discount offer popup on exit
10. **Personalization**: Customization options for orders

## Layout Patterns

### Product Grid
- 4-6 columns on desktop
- 2-3 columns on tablet
- 1-2 columns on mobile
- Card-based design with image, title, price, discount badge

### Product Card Elements
- Product image (with hover zoom)
- Product title
- Price (sale and original)
- Discount badge
- Quick add-to-cart button
- Quick view option

### Checkout Flow
1. Cart review
2. Shipping address
3. Shipping method selection
4. Payment method selection
5. Order confirmation
